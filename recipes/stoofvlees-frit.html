Stoofvlees Frit

The national dish of Belgium

A simple meat stew made with beef and beer. Served with fries and mayonaise

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Beer
  • Fries
  • mayonaise

Steps

  1. Prepare the stew by stewing the beef in beer
  2. Allow it to slowly cook over a couple of hours
  3. Cut up and fry homemade fries
  4. serve with delicious mayonaise
